Privacy Policy for Stack Chaos

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stack Chaos ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play Stack Chaos. However, certain information may be collected automatically or through third-party services integrated into the App:

  • Gameplay data: your scores, coin balances, and in-app progress, stored locally on your device.
  • Device and advertising identifiers: used by our advertising partner (Google AdMob) to serve and measure ads, subject to your consent.
  • Purchase information: if you make an in-app purchase, payment is processed entirely by Apple's App Store; we do not receive or store your payment card details. Purchase validation is handled through RevenueCat, our subscription and purchase management provider.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy practices:

  • Google AdMob — displays interstitial and rewarded video advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — manages and validates in-app purchases. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — processes all payment transactions for in-app purchases.

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, the App will request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites, in compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You may grant or deny this permission at any time via your device's Settings.

4. Children's Privacy

Stack Chaos is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can disable ad tracking via your device's Settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking), reset your device's advertising identifier at any time, or contact us with any privacy-related requests.

California residents: You may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion.

6. Data Retention

Gameplay data (scores, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App. We do not maintain a central server database of individual player data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
